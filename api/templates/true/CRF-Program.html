How to apply and receive your Fintech Community Relief Payments

The stages of receiving your Fintech Community Relief Funds for business, self-employed individuals, or independent contractors are described below:

  • Submit your Preliminary Form to the Finance Desk
  • Receive your Fintech secure QR Barcode
  • Contact your appointed Finance Officer (Training on how to complete your community relief deposit at the Digital Currency Machine and mobile dispatch application for the first time)
  • Receive your first FCR payment in your bank account and complete your first dispatch duty immediately using your QR barcode.

After completing your first dispatch duty, you will be experienced in completing your future duties with the assistance of your Finance Officer.

APPLY NOW

To qualify for the Community Relief Funds for citizens of the United States of America, you must:

  • Live in the US or its territories
  • Be available for manual verification of identification and banking information, and completion of dispatch duties at every payment cycle.
  • Operate a highly credible bank account where your community relief funds will be paid recurrently. If you do not have a bank account, you may request financial advice from the Finance Desk on the best financial institution to open an account within your community.
  • Be available during a community relief dispatch cycle to fund the Digital Currency Machine closest to your home address. Performance during each cycle will influence improvement in your personal and business credit scores to GOOD/EXCELLENT within one calendar year.
